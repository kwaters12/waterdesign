 Introduction:
  I moved to Vancouver in 2005 to study Game Design.
 In September 2007 I went back to school at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. We did everything from designing websites, wayfaring systems and kinetic sculptures to spatial and product design.
  Following graduation, I found a job game testing at United Front Games. After working there for 4 months, I was promoted to a World Artist, and really thrived in that position.
 Near the end of my time at United Front Games I was given an offer to move to Beijing, China and work as a 3D Designer for Dream Lighting City Culture Development Co. Ltd. It was an amazing experience immersing myself in a foreign culture. I worked there for a year, and had a lot of interesting experiences, including being interviewed by Phoenix News from Hong Kong, signing a contract with the local government in Xi'An with 100+ cameras there, and spending a lot of time exploring the amazing 797 Art Zone as well as the Beijing design scene (A LOT of interesting stuff going on there.)

 Future Plan:
  I want to work on amazing projects with amazing people. I spend a lot of my time studying design and design software, and I feel like I have barely scratched the surface of what I want to create and add to this world.
